Heading tags, consisting of h1 - h6 with h1 being the largest.

The largest heading should be saved for the title of a page.

Where smaller heading elements are good for sections.

Paragraph tags are nested within the body tag.

Line spacing can be done with paragraph tags.

Bold text with the Strong Element.

em Element is used for Italics/Emphasis

Nesting

Nesting elements inside other elements creates a Parent Child relationship, where the elements inside are the children.
